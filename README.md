## Important Notes on Data and Roles

* **General Guideline & Scope:** The roles listed here are intended as broad categories. Highly specialized areas, such as **Cloud Services (AWS, Azure)** and **Platform Development (Salesforce, SAP)**, contain numerous distinct job titles (e.g., Solutions Architect, Cloud Security Engineer, Platform Administrator) that all draw on the core KSA provided. These specialized positions often require **multiple, vendor-specific certifications** to qualify.
* **Dynamic Field:** E-commerce and technology fields are highly dynamic. These skills and associated pay scales **change rapidly**. Salaries are estimates as of **early 2025**, and actual pay is heavily influenced by company size, experience, specific location, and negotiation skills.
* **Career Mobility:** Modern IT professionals typically change jobs every **2-4 years** to maximize salary growth (15-30% increases) and skill acquisition. See [Career_Trends.md](Career_Trends.md) for strategic guidance.
* **Growth Figures:** Growth rates are primarily sourced from the US Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS) 2024-2034 projections and major industry reports. They are **estimates of future demand** and are provided with corresponding links in the **References** section.
* **Education/Training:** While a Bachelor's Degree is listed as typical for many roles, experience, a strong portfolio, and relevant certifications often substitute or are weighed more heavily in the tech industry.
* **Technical Writing:** Across nearly all roles, **technical writing and communication skills** are increasingly critical, especially for AI/ML positions where prompt engineering and model documentation are essential.
